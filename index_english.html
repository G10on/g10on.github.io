About me

I am a Computer Science Engineer graduated from the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria (ULPGC). My ability and passion for learning in the world of technologies and software have given me the interest to contribute and innovate in solutions of any magnitude.

Since high school I had already gained experience in teamwork, participating in 2 competitions with a robotics team, and having been part of a basketball team. That's why, when faced with new projects, I consider them an opportunity to get to know people with different specialties and perspectives, from which I am fascinated to learn new things during the exchange of knowledge that takes place among all the team members.

My obsession for simplicity and efficiency of solutions in a visual way has allowed me to obtain a great resolutive capacity. With my interest in understanding the effects and impact of projects in the world, I have acquired the ability to make extensive explorations of all the issues in which they are involved.

My goal is to contribute to the development of software of all kinds and to innovate in solutions that change the way of life of everyone, from solutions for companies in many different areas, to research and development in science research and new technologies.
